About the Project

The World Integrated Trade Solution (WITS) website provides access to international merchandise trade, tariff and non-tariff measures (NTM) data. However, the presentation of aggregate data on this site is not very intuitive. Our team saw this as an opportunity to use World Bank data and present that information in a way that is more accessible to a general audience while continuing to maintain the original integrity of the WITS website.

Our team, composed of Paul Bernert, Hans Engelbrecht and Sam Ettenson, welcome you to explore this site and explore the different methods we chose for presenting international trade data.
